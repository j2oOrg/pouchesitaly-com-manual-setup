HOW DO I USE SNUS?

Nicotine pouches are placed between the (typically upper) lip and gums, providing almost immediate nicotine effects. They come in various strengths, ranging from as little as 2 milligrams to over 50 milligrams of nicotine per pouch, so the choice of product matters significantly.

Manufacturers suggest using nicotine pouches for 15 to 60 minutes. The strongest effects usually wear off within 30 minutes. Swallowing nicotine pouches can harm you which is why you should avoi it. Medical professionals advise to remove the pouch immediately if experiencing any adverse effects like nausea or dizziness, keeping medical advice accessible and the product out of children’s reach.
